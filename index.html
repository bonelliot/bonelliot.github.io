Ingredient Information

Performance Ingredients
  • AMBR Complex™
    Our proprietary combination of pre and probiotics, skin identical ceramides and optimised antioxidants designed to rebalance the skin's natural health networks. For self optimising, resilient and adaptable skin.
  • Bioretinoid alternative
    New generation microalgae derived bioretinoid acts as a natural retinoic acid analogue, with liposomal encapsulation for enhanced delivery. For effective cell regulation and renewal without irritation.
  • Peptides
    Intuitive peptides selectively act on neuronal cellular signalling pathways, reducing SNARE protein formation to soften the appearance of lines. For improved texture and firmness.
  • Vitamin C
    AA2G (Ascorbal Glucaside). A stabilised, controlled release form of vitamin C, highly effective at non-irritating pH and concentrations (unlike L-Ascorbic acid). For maximal luminosity without irritation.
  • Niacinamide
    High-graded niacinamide contains the purest form of the vitamin B3 molecule, without the elevated amounts of micro inflammatory nicotinic acid found in lower grade forms of niacinamide. For skin clarity and resilience.
  • Hyaluronic acid
    Multi-weight hyaluronic acid attracts and binds water molecules to hydrate throughout the layers of the epidermis from superficial to deep. For immediate and lasting hydration.
  • Hexylresorcinol
    Powerful antioxidant protects against free radical damage and glycation (the process of sugar molecules damaging collagen and elastin) at a cellular level. For firming and evening skin tone.
  • Licorice root
    Natural antioxidants including Licochalcone A protect against free radical damage at a cellular level, reducing erythema, dryness and inflammation. For calmer skin.
  • Squalane
    Olive-derived squalane mimics the skin’s own natural moisturising factors, readily incorporated into the stratum corneum for building and strengthening the barrier. For increased hydration and protection.
  • Panthenol
    Provitamin B5 derivative promotes epidermal cell regulation and lipid synthesis, reinforcing the skin barrier and reducing trans epidermal water loss. For soothed skin.

BEHIND THE BRAND

Bon Elliot’s origins are in Australia, with a distinctly global perspective. The brand shares the name of Bonnie and our family team of Australian co-founders, working across Sydney and New York.

“I've had the privilege of helping tens of thousands of patients with skin impacted by Australia's unforgiving conditions.” says Dr Bonnie Fergie.

“As I reflected on the common experience of skin becoming increasingly damaged and sensitive over time, I began to search for a path that maximised outer form by prioritising the daily and long-term health of the skin, rooted in the science of the skin’s physiology.”

“What started over 5 years ago has culminated in AMBR Complex™, our patent pending complex to rebalance the skin’s natural health networks — at the foundation of our formulas” says Bonnie of her research.

Our formulas are proudly made in Sydney, benefiting from Australia’s clean manufacturing environment and rigorous standards, while being proven with leading international independent laboratory clinical trials and including Next Generation gene sequencing of the microbiome.

The brand carries the DNA of our family co-founders behind award-winning New York based creative studio Little Troop, known for its culturally resonant and forward-looking design.

Co-founder and creative director Noemie Le Coz, recently inducted into the prestigious Alliance Graphique Internationale, says “As with our formulas, the visual identity of Bon Elliot lies in the skin’s physiology and future-facing lens to skincare that is at the heart of our brand.”

“The unique visual elements of Bon Elliot are a reflection of the depth of our science and craftsmanship of our product, while bearing all the hallmarks of new luxury.”

“Finally, our Australian origins are core to who we are and we wanted that to be true in every aspect. Our launch campaign, inspired by ‘Skin health under the microscope’, was shot in New York with all Australian talent, and alongside accomplished Sydney-born set designer Alicia Sciberras.”

“While the brand has been designed from two opposite sides of the globe, I was proud to be able to bring an incredible team of Australians together for our launch campaign.”
